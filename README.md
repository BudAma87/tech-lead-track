# Tech Lead Track — Role-Based Training

> **Track:** Tech Lead
> **Participant:** Buddhika
> **Program length:** 5 months · 4 hours per session · Advanced difficulty
> **Structure:** Each month = one facilitated session (3-hour challenge) + offline milestones

A five-month leadership development program that builds the capabilities of a
Tech Lead across four pillars: **strategic architecture**, **people
management**, **project delivery**, **cross-functional influence**, and
**advanced technical leadership**. Every month centers on a realistic business
scenario and produces four graded deliverables (25 points each, 100 total,
**75% passing**).

---

## Program at a Glance

| Month | Theme | Running Scenario | Focus |
|-------|-------|------------------|-------|

| [5](month-5-advanced-tech-leadership/) | Advanced Tech Leadership | Setting technical direction for a team growing 8 → 15 | Tech vision, innovation culture, team charter, strategy |

---

## Learning Arc

The track moves deliberately from **systems** to **people** to **execution** to
**influence** to **direction** — the full span of what a Tech Lead owns:

1. **Month 1 — Think strategically.** Shape enterprise architecture and align
   technology investment with business goals.
2. **Month 2 — Lead people.** Grow, review, and support a team with empathy and
   a high bar.
3. **Month 3 — Deliver under pressure.** Drive a high-stakes project with honest
   planning and risk management.
4. **Month 4 — Influence without authority.** Lead across organizational
   boundaries where you have responsibility but not control.
5. **Month 5 — Set direction.** Craft vision, build an innovation culture, and
   codify how the team works.

## Scoring

Every month is scored out of 100 points (four deliverables × 25). The same
rubric applies across the track:

| Grade | Score | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Exceptional | 90–100 | Executive/manager-ready, comprehensive |
| Proficient | 75–89 | Solid work, minor gaps |
| Developing | 60–74 | Core elements present, needs work |
| Beginning | <60 | Significant gaps, requires rework |

**Passing score:** 75% per month.

## Key Frameworks & Resources Across the Track

- **Architecture:** C4 Model, Architecture Decision Records (ADRs), TOGAF, ThoughtWorks Technology Radar
- **People:** SBI + Request feedback, What + How reviews, Radical Candor, *The Manager's Path*
- **Delivery:** 5×5 risk matrix, critical-path method, capacity/velocity planning, *The Phoenix Project*
- **Influence:** Power/Interest grid, Cialdini's *Influence*, *Crucial Conversations*, *Getting to Yes*
- **Direction:** Technology vision, 70/20/10 innovation model, team charters, *Staff Engineer* (Will Larson)
