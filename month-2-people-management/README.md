# Month 2 — People Management & Team Leadership

> **Track:** Tech Lead
> **Duration:** 4 hours
> **Focus:** 1:1s, performance reviews, feedback, career development
> **Difficulty:** Advanced

---

## Session Overview

This session develops the people-leadership skills required of a Tech Lead:
running effective 1:1s, writing fair and specific performance reviews, delivering
feedback that changes behavior, and building career development plans that grow
the team. The emphasis is on being both **supportive and rigorous** — caring for
people while holding a high bar.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:

- Run structured, individualized 1:1 meetings
- Write comprehensive, balanced performance reviews
- Deliver effective feedback using proven frameworks (SBI)
- Build actionable career development plans
- Support underperformers with empathy and clear expectations

---

## Core Concepts

### The Tech Lead's People Responsibilities

| Responsibility | What Good Looks Like |
|----------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| 1:1s | Regular, employee-driven, action-oriented |
| Feedback | Specific, timely, behavior-focused, actionable |
| Performance | Fair, evidence-based, balanced (What + How) |
| Career growth | Individualized plans with real support |
| Difficult situations | Empathy + clear expectations + a path forward |

### The Meet Your Team

You lead a team with a range of situations:

| Team Member | Situation |
|-------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Alex | High performer, aspiring Tech Lead |
| Jordan | Developing engineer, strong learning agility |
| Riley | Underperforming due to personal circumstances |
| Sam | Skilled but stays in comfort zone |
| Morgan | Reliable, collaborative team glue |
| Casey | Junior, building independence |

### Frameworks Used

- **SBI + Request:** Situation → Behavior → Impact → Request
- **What + How:** results (What) balanced with behaviors (How)
- **Growth mindset:** development areas framed as opportunities

## Facilitator Notes

### Common Pitfalls

- Turning 1:1s into status updates instead of people conversations
- Vague, non-actionable feedback ("be more proactive")
- Recency bias in performance reviews
- One-size-fits-all career plans
- Avoiding difficult conversations until too late

### Discussion Prompts

- "How do you balance empathy with accountability?"
- "What makes feedback actually change behavior?"
- "How do you support an underperformer fairly to them and the team?"

# Challenge: Leading a Growing Team

> **Time Allocation:** 3 hours (during session)
> **Difficulty:** Advanced

You are the **Tech Lead** of a six-person engineering team with a mix of
performance levels, career aspirations, and personal situations. Produce the
people-management artifacts a strong Tech Lead would use to run 1:1s, review
performance, deliver feedback, and grow careers.

### 1. 1:1 Meeting Templates (25 points)

**File:** `{your-name}-month2-1on1.md`

Required: a standard 1:1 template (prep, structure, post-meeting), customized
templates for at least 3 team members, and worked 1:1 note examples.

**Evaluation Criteria**
- Template completeness (5 pts)
- Customization quality (5 pts)
- Question effectiveness (5 pts)
- Action orientation (5 pts)
- Follow-up structure (5 pts)

---

### 2. Performance Review Documentation (25 points)

**File:** `{your-name}-month2-performance.md`

Required: **2 full performance reviews** covering Results (What), Behaviors (How),
growth assessment, and future focus.

**Evaluation Criteria**
- Comprehensive assessment (5 pts)
- Balanced feedback (5 pts)
- Specific examples (5 pts)
- Development focus (5 pts)
- Professional tone (5 pts)

---

### 3. Feedback Frameworks (25 points)

**File:** `{your-name}-month2-feedback.md`

Required: **6 feedback scenarios** using SBI + Request (positive, constructive,
difficult, peer conflict, growth, recognition).

**Evaluation Criteria**
- SBI framework application (5 pts)
- Specific examples (5 pts)
- Appropriate tone (5 pts)
- Actionable requests (5 pts)
- Scenario variety (5 pts)

---

### 4. Career Development Plans (25 points)

**File:** `{your-name}-month2-career.md`

Required: **2 career development plans** with aspirations, gap analysis,
quarter-by-quarter actions, support needed, and check-in cadence.

**Evaluation Criteria**
- Goal clarity (5 pts)
- Gap analysis accuracy (5 pts)
- Action plan specificity (5 pts)
- Timeline realism (5 pts)
- Support identification (5 pts)
